How it works

Easily create a product category from the dashboard after signing in

Click on the 'Add Category' button located in your dashboard to add a product category.

Add a sale record for each category from the dashboard

Click on the Plus Sign located on the category in your dashboard to add a sale(s) record for that category.

Easily edit a sale record

Click on the edit icon button located on the right of the records table under the 'Actions' column to edit a sale record.

Easily delete a sale record

Click on the delete icon button located on the right of the records table under the 'Actions' column to delete a sale record.
